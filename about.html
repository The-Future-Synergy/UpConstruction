Our Story

Founded in 1988, UPCONSTRUCTION has grown from a small local contractor into a trusted leader in construction and infrastructure development. With over three decades of excellence, we specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial projects, delivering quality craftsmanship and innovative designs on time and within budget.

  • Over 35 years of trusted construction experience
  • Expert team of engineers, architects, and project managers
  • Commitment to safety, quality, and sustainability

At UPCONSTRUCTION, our mission is to build strong foundations both in structures and in relationships. Every project we take on reflects our dedication to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction. From concept to completion, we turn your vision into reality with precision and professionalism.
